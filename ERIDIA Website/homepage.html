We are devoted to providing the best possible tutoring service to our clients and as part of that we create customisable lesson plans based upon you or your child's own specific learning style. To determine what your learning style is we use a process called the VARK system, whereby the tutee fills in a simple personality quiz style questionnaire. There are no right or wrong answers, it simply determines whether they are a Visual Learner, an Auditory Learner, a Reading and Writing Learner or if they are a Kinaesthetic Learner.

Some people will only be one type of learner and some people will have a combined learning type. We can also use the results from this quiz to customise revision resources that will best suit them. For example, a Visual Learner might benefit from visual aids like posters or mind maps, whereas an Auditory Learner may like to voice record their notes and play them back at a later date to refresh their memory. With kinaesthetic learners it is a little bit more challenging because they learn by doing things and by physically being involved in the learning process. For these type of learners we might produce flashcard resources or use game-based revision exercises in our lessons together.
